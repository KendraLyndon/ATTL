The Story

Bethlehem is abuzz at the return of Naomi to her hometown - ten years after her husband moved her and their two sons to neighboring (and despised) Moab. She returns penniless, childless, and a widow. She also brings her Moabite daughter-in-law, Ruth, who wants desperately to belong to this community and to help her beloved mother-in-law.

Naomi is viewed as having 'gone over to the enemy' for the move to Moab. Ruth is treated with suspicion and harsh prejudice. Wicked and miserly Uncle Nemuel wants Naomi's land for his own and will stop at nothing to get it. Naomi is determined not only to keep the land but also to build a heritage for herself and her devoted but headstrong daughter-in-law.

But then a most unlikely suitor, Boaz, falls in love with Ruth...and what could be the women's salvation could also bring about their undoing.
